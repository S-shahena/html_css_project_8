Description

Hotell is a simple hotel website template with an engaging, modern and creative appearance. Keep in mind, Hotell works flawlessly for other accommodation businesses as well, so no need to feel limited in any way. While you can work with Hotell as is, you can also configure its default settings, altering and branding it accordingly.


Promote your must-visit location with a well-thought-out website and win over more potential customers. Hotell is here to leave a strong and lasting impression on everyone. Share all the ins and outs of your hotel or resort, share promotional video, write a compelling about page, whatever, it’s all possible with Hotell. The contact page also comes with a form, and you can even activate the Instagram-ready feed.
